About the project

Nowadays you can't get around a website without a content management system. Due to the use of these different standard systems for many years, we’ve developed Bakku with one of our partners (Noppo). Bakku only shows the users the essential functionality they will need. It is a content management system build for the customer. Our users say it's the most simple system they have ever used to update their website.
